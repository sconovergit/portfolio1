About Me

My name is Alex Cooper, I am in my 30’s and love photography. I do this as a hobby of mine because I love interacting with people and enjoy capturing important moments in people’s lives.

Born and raised in Buffalo, NY I have always had an interest in photography, but did not act on it until later in life. After working in IT all day I wanted a way to tap into my creativity and that’s when photography really became a great hobby for me.

I look forward to hearing from you, and please do not hesitate to contact me. Thank you for checking out my site and work and I hope to meet you soon!

-Alex Cooper
